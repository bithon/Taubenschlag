Join FLO Retweets on Twitter!

The FLO Retweets Bot connects to your twitter account and automatically retweets everything the official FLO twitter tweets!

You can also set it to tweet less frequently if you don't want to retweet everything.

The bot just retweets, please keep liking good tweets!

Please sign up using your twitter account on https://retweets.floblockchain.com/oAuthTwitter/start - after the authorization you will receive a Direct Message on Twitter with further information.

View the FLO Retweets Bot source code on GitHub!
